Weekly Pipeline Change Analysis

Understand how your open weighted sales pipeline in Dynamics CRM changes week over week.

What This App Does

  • New Track new opportunities entering the pipeline each week
  • Won See which deals were closed and won
  • Lost Identify lost opportunities and their impact
  • Changes Review weighted value increases and decreases on open deals

Split by opportunity type: System Integration (CE) and Managed Services

Seeds demo users and pipeline data for testing
